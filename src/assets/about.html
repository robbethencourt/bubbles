Daily Bubbles

Increase your daily water intake each time you finish your water bottle. It's that simple. One click to increase your daily intake, and don't worry, you can easily decrease by the same amount if you need to.

How to Track Your Daily Water Intake

As you hydrate throughout the day the bubble outline will go from pink to blue. And when you reach your goal for the day you'll see a congratulatory bubbles animation!

Bubbles, You're So Clever!

But how does Bubbles know how big my water bottle is? And how does bubbles know what my water intake goal is for the day?

My Settings

You can quickly adjust your water bottle size and your daily goal on the settings page. Bubbles will remember them once you update them. Just set it and start hydrating.

But I Don't Always Drink from My Water Bottle

So true. Sometimes you're at lunch and you need to adjust your intake a few ounces at a time. You can do that on the Navigation page.

Navigation

Just click on the nav icon ☰ on the top right and quickly adjust your intake 2 oz at a time. You can also reach the settings page and the daily water tracking page through the nav.

The About link in the nav will bring you back to this page if you ever want to refresh yourself on how to use bubbles.
